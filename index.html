Who We Are

More Than a Game.
A Path Forward.

Playmaker Sports Foundation is dedicated to expanding access to youth sports and personal development opportunities through athletic participation, educational programming, scholarships, leadership development, and community-based initiatives.

Through soccer and other athletic opportunities, the Foundation empowers young athletes with pathways to compete, learn, grow, and succeed — both on and off the field.

Our Mission

Playmaker Sports Foundation is dedicated to expanding access to youth sports and personal development opportunities through athletic participation, educational programming, scholarships, leadership development, and community-based initiatives.

The Foundation seeks to empower young athletes by providing pathways to compete, learn, grow, and succeed both on and off the field. Through soccer and other athletic opportunities, we promote character development, teamwork, leadership, sportsmanship, and lifelong success.

To create a future where every young athlete has access to meaningful opportunities to participate, develop, compete, and succeed — regardless of financial circumstances.

What We Do

Programs Built for
Every Athlete

Each initiative is designed to remove barriers, build character, and create lasting opportunity for youth athletes in our community.

🎓

Youth Athlete Scholarship Program

Provides financial assistance to youth athletes and families who may not otherwise have access to athletic participation, training, educational experiences, or competitive events.

⚽

Community Soccer Clinics

Free and low-cost soccer clinics that promote physical activity, skill development, teamwork, sportsmanship, and meaningful community engagement for all skill levels.

📋

Coaching Education Initiative

Supports coaches through educational workshops, mentorship opportunities, and leadership development resources that measurably improve the youth sports experience.

🏅

Leadership Through Sport

Uses athletics as a platform to teach leadership, character development, accountability, teamwork, communication, and essential life skills young people carry beyond the field.

🎽

Equipment Assistance Program

Provides sports equipment and resources to youth athletes facing financial barriers to participation — because no athlete should sit out due to lack of gear.
